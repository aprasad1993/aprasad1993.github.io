Basmati Rice

Basmati rice is one of the most well-known and internationally recognized geographic indications that comes from Indian sub-continent. Basmati rice has certain unique characteristics, which owe their origin to the genotype and special agro-climatic conditions prevailing in the Indo- Gangetic Plains.
